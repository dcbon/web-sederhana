Our Product

Balthazar Blend Organic

A mélange of three very special coffees from Africa, Central America and Asia. Hand roasted to a full French Roast allowing the robust body, dark chocolate, cedar, orange zest tones, and elegant finish to captivate your senses.

Berkeley Blend

A dark roasted blend of Indonesian, Ethiopian, Central and South American coffees. Rich and smooth with a balanced taste. Complex in the cup. Excellent also as an espresso drink.

European Royale

European Royale is one of most popular dark roasts. This complex blend brings beans from 3 continents together into one balanced cup. It begins full, mellows as it lingers and then finishes with a smooth sweet aftertaste.

Espresso Nuovo

Our Espresso Nuovo is a rich blend of Central and South America beans combined with Indonesia beans that are roasted to a deep dark brown. The result is a complex full-bodied brew with a thick rich cream.

Majestic Blend

Our Majestic Blend is a superb blend of African, Central and South American beans. This “regal” dark roast is a rich, complex brew, with the African beans adding a soft, sweet aftertaste.

Moka Java Dark

One of our most unique blends marries 2 very traditional coffees together to create a magical blend. Pungent fruit, complex aromatics and a balanced finish make this a coffee that’s sure to please.

Heritage Blend

Created with coffee beans from our direct farm relationships in Costa Rica, Ethiopia and Guatemala combined to create a rich, full-bodied cup with a soft finish. Tasting notes of milk chocolate, dried fig and hazelnut, roasted to a light brown.

Hawaiian Kona Fancy

lixir of Royalty! Hawaiian Imperial Kona Fancy delivers an old world mellow body with a balanced sweetness. Aromatic coffee with character that plays between acidity and sweetness, is slightly spicy, with overtones of cinnamon and cloves. 1-lb bags.
